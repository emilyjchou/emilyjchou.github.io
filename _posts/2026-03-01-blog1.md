**Who am I?**

My name is Emily Chou, and I am an international student from Taiwan currently majoring in Computer Science at SFSU. 

As someone who has experience in creating websites, I always try to focus on what layout, feature, and placement would be the best for my intended users. Whenever I come across a new website, I like to think about how this website is doing well and what it lacks design-wise. Over time, I’ve come to understand that good design is just as important as good code.
Learning about Human-Computer Interaction will help me take that mindset further. Instead of relying on instinct alone, I want to base my design decisions on research, user behavior, and proven principles. I want my projects to not only function properly but also provide the best possible experience for the people using them.
